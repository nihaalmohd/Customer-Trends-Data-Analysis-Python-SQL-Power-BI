# Customer-Trends-Data-Analysis-Python-SQL-Power-BI
📌 **Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping patterns to uncover key business insights and enhance decision-making using data analytics. It combines SQL, Python, and Power BI with data-driven insights to simulate a real-world retail analytics solution.  The goal is to understand customer behavior, purchasing trends, and revenue drivers.

🎯 **Objectives**
-Analyze customer purchase behavior and trends
-Identify high-value customers and key revenue segments
-Perform data cleaning and transformation using SQL & Python
-Build interactive dashboards for business stakeholders
-Integrate Generative AI to generate automated insights and summaries

🧰 **Tech Stack**
-SQL – Data extraction, transformation, and analysis
-Python (Pandas, NumPy) – Data cleaning and preprocessing
-Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization
-PostgreSQL – Database management
-Generative AI (OpenAI API) – Insight generation and reporting

📊 **Key Features**
-Customer segmentation based on spending behavior
-Trend analysis of purchases over time
-Product/category performance insights
-KPI tracking (Revenue, Orders, Customer Retention)
-Automated data-to-insight pipeline

📈 **Dashboard Highlights**
-Revenue trends and growth analysis
-Top-performing products and categories
-Customer segmentation visuals
-Region-wise or demographic insights

🚀 **Project Workflow**
1.Data Collection & Storage (PostgreSQL)
2.Data Cleaning & Transformation (SQL + Python)
3.Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
4.Dashboard Development (Power BI)
5.Final Reporting & Visualization

💡 **Business Impact**
-Helps businesses understand customer purchasing patterns
-Supports data-driven decision making
-Reduces manual effort in generating reports using AI
-Improves customer targeting and marketing strategies
